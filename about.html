What is BlogBook ?

BlogBook is a Blog Application whwre yiu can create, edit, delete and update posts and blogs.

What you can do on the app?

When you sign in to your Account, you can browse, edit, and delete published and draft posts. If you have admin or author permissions to multiple blogs, you can switch between blogs.

With this app you can

  • Create and publish new posts
  • Share a link to your post
  • Switch between accounts and blogs
  • Visit the BlogBook Help Center
  • Send in-app feedback
